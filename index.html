How to Play
  • 1
    Each team tells the quizmaster a category and a difficulty level (1–5 points) to select their question.
  • 2
    Correct answer — the team earns points equal to the difficulty level chosen.
  • 3
    Wrong answer — the team loses points equal to the difficulty level chosen.
  • 4
    Once a question is selected it is removed from the board and cannot be chosen again.
  • 5
    The quiz is played over 3 rounds. The team with the most points at the end wins!
